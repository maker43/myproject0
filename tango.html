Belgrade!






"Dance like no one's watching."

Belgrade is city of all of its inhabitants, and while a lot of them represent Belgrade in right ways we beliave that comunity of "Tango Natural" represents Belgrade at its fullest. As stated on their website:

"Our mission:

Blooming through tango Every day we find pleasure in the blooming of those who chose tango in order to dance through their lives, and partaking in this process stands for our main satisfaction. We spread tango because we are grateful to it for everything that it has brought to us, and on this path we acquire help from many of those who share this gratitude with us. We are joyful for the development of the tango community – a place of ever-lasting adventure and true values."

~Tango Natural

Before break out of COVID-19 "Tango Encuentro Belgrade" was organised every year with ever growing audience. And it became one of the largest Tango festivals in Europe, thanks to the energy that Tango community in Belgrade possess and shares wholeheartedly with everyone. We hope that it will continue where it left off...

~Tango Encuentro
